About This Site

The VOLANO™ chat software for the Java™ platform lets you build on-line communities and add real-time social networking to your Web site. Since it was introduced in December 1996, the VOLANO chat server has been sold to customers in 50 countries around the world.

John Neffenger is the author of the VOLANO chat software and is doing business as Status Six Communications in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
